How it works

Simple as a round of drinks.

Brewskr gets out of your way and gets you into the room.

01 🍺
Post a Shoutout

You're at The Rusty Nail. Tap "Shout!" and let your circle know — "We're here, come through." One tap. Done.

02 📍
They See the Vibe

Your circle gets a push notification. Nearby open circles show up on the Good Vibes Map, filtered by shared interests.

03 📲
Tap to Connect

Meet someone new? Tap phones together (NFC) or scan a QR code. Instant connection. Shared interests revealed on screen.

04 🎉
Good Vibes Happen

Real conversations. Real memories. No algorithm needed. This is how we used to meet people — now with a little help.

Trust & Safety

Built to be safe
from the ground up.

Real-world connection comes with real-world responsibility. We take that seriously.

  • 👤
    Handles, Not Real Names

    You're @whoever you want to be. Your real name, email, and phone are never visible to other users.

  • 📍
    Location Privacy by Default

    The map is OFF by default. When you opt in, other users see your venue — not your precise GPS coordinates.

  • 🛡️
    Block, Report, Protect

    One-tap blocking and reporting. Human moderation. We take every report seriously.

  • 🚗
    Safe Ride Integration

    Emergency ride prompt after 4 hours at a bar. Designated Driver badge earns monthly credits.

  • 🔒
    No Ads. No Data Sales.

    We will never sell your personal data. We will never show you ads. Our business model is subscriptions — your interests are not for sale.
